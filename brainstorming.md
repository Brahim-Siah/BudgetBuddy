## New App Ideas – Evaluate and Categorize

### 1. BudgetBuddy

**Description:**  
A personal finance tracker that automatically categorizes spending, shows visual insights, and helps users set and maintain savings goals. The app breaks down expenses into simple categories and provides easy-to-read charts.

**Category:**  
Finance / Productivity

**Mobile:**  
Mobile allows for real-time notifications on spending, quick receipt scanning through the camera, and instant updates whenever a transaction occurs.

**Story:**  
Helps users understand where their money goes, improve budgeting habits, and stay on track with savings goals through smart reminders and visual spending summaries.

**Market:**  
Anyone who earns, spends, or saves money could use this app — especially students, young professionals, and families trying to organize their finances.

**Habit:**  
Users may check the app daily or weekly to view spending updates, confirm categories, and review their savings progress.

**Scope:**  
- **V1:** Basic income/expense entry, categories, and charts  
- **V2:** Automatic bank syncing, savings goals, and alerts  
- **V3:** Shared budgets and bill reminders

### 2. StudySync

**Description:**  
A collaboration and study-organizing tool for students. Allows classmates to share notes, flashcards, reminders, and a shared assignment calendar.

**Category:**  
Education / Social Collaboration

**Mobile:**  
Students can quickly add reminders, review notes on the go, and get push alerts for due dates or study sessions.

**Story:**  
Makes studying more organized and social by connecting classmates and helping them stay aligned on assignments and exam preparation.

**Market:**  
Middle school, high school, and college students — especially group project teams or study groups.

**Habit:**  
Students may check the app multiple times per day for schedule updates, shared notes, or flashcards.

**Scope:**  
- **V1:** Notes, reminders, and a basic shared calendar  
- **V2:** Flashcards, file uploads, and class groups  
- **V3:** Messaging or tutor matching

---

### 3. Password Safe

**Description:**  
A secure password manager where each password is stored behind a master passcode, with entries hidden until unlocked.

**Category:**  
Security / Utility

**Mobile:**  
Mobile allows quick access to passwords, biometric unlocking, and on-device encrypted storage.

**Story:**  
Helps users manage and protect dozens of passwords they can’t memorize while avoiding unsafe habits like storing passwords in notes.

**Market:**  
Anyone with online accounts — especially students, professionals, and frequent internet users.

**Habit:**  
Users typically open the app whenever they need to log in to accounts or update passwords.

**Scope:**  
- **V1:** Encrypted password storage and a master lock  
- **V2:** Categories, password strength analysis, and auto-generate passwords  
- **V3:** Cloud backup or multi-device sync

## ⭐ Final App Selection: BudgetBuddy

### Final App Choice – BudgetBuddy (Full Breakdown)

**Category:**  
Finance / Productivity

**Mobile:**  
Mobile provides real-time financial tracking and instant updates. Users can take photos of receipts, categorize expenses quickly, and receive timely notifications about overspending or goal progress.

**Story:**  
BudgetBuddy helps users gain control of their finances by breaking down spending into categories and displaying simple graphs. It guides users toward better habits with savings suggestions, reminders, and easy-to-understand visuals.

**Market:**  
BudgetBuddy serves students, families, and professionals who want a clear picture of their finances. The app appeals to people who want lightweight budgeting without complicated spreadsheets or expensive premium tools.

**Habit:**  
Users typically check the app weekly or after making a purchase. The receipt-scanning feature and daily spending alerts encourage regular engagement.

**Scope:**  
- **V1:** Expense entry, categories, spending summaries, charts  
- **V2:** Savings goals, reminders, simple analytics, receipt scanning  
- **V3:** Bank sync, shared budgets, bill reminders, or AI-powered suggestions
